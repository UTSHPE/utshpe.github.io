Meet UT-SHPE's 2023-2024 Leadership Team!

These are the amazing, determined individuals who make up our leaderSHPE (get it?) team! Each of them are tasked with vital responsibilities that not only sustain our chapter’s vision and mission, but also lead by example with their perseverance and dedication.

Executive Board

Rogelio J Mendoza
He/Him
President
Mechanical Engineering
Why I joined SHPE:
I joined SHPE for the familia I was able to find on my first day. I have met great friends and mentors which allowed me to find a strong sense of belonging. Now it's my turn to give back to the bright cohorts of SHPE members coming after me!
Favorite SHPE Memory:
My favorite SHPE memory has been E-Week 2023 when my friends and I went to Houston for e-week challenges. It was a super fun weekend with lots of great memories that I will cherish forever for years to come.
Zaida Flores
She/Her
Vice President External
Electrical & Computer Engineering
Why I joined SHPE:
I joined SHPE because I missed being around Hispanic culture. Growing up in a border town, I was in a bubble. Once I got to UT, it was both intimidating and amazing being around so many different people and cultures. Although I was learning and experiencing so much, I quickly realized I missed the feeling of community.
Favorite SHPE Memory:
My favorite SHPE memory is the fall LeaderSHPE retreat. Not only was it great getting to know my fellow LeaderSHPE members, but I made friends out of all of them. Playing games, joking around, and making family meals brought us all really close together. Having a moment to slow down and bond with everyone outside of school was really special.
